About Me

I am a game designer and educator with a passion for creating engaging interactive experiences that blend physical and digital play. My work focuses on tabletop games, augmented reality (AR) experiences, and mixed media game design.

As an instructor in the Media Arts & Technologies Department at Montgomery College, I teach TECH 295: Tabletop and Mixed Media Game Design, where students explore the history, theory, and practice of non-digital and hybrid game development. My goal is to help students build the creative and technical skills they need to succeed in the game industry.

I believe that games are powerful tools for storytelling, education, and community building. Whether designing an AR escape room, a narrative board game, or a puzzle-based scavenger hunt, I aim to create experiences that bring people together and challenge them to think creatively.
